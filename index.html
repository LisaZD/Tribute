Marcella Hazan

Classic Italian Chef and Cookbook Author

Marcella is best known for teaching classic Italian cooking techniques and making the cuisine accessible to American home cooks.

Biographical Timeline

  • 1924: Born in the Italian town of Cesenatico in Emilia-Romagna
  • 1955: Married Victor Hazan and moved to New York City
  • 1969: Began teaching cooking classes in her apartment
  • 1973: Published her first book, The Classic Italian Cookbook
  • 1978: Published More Classic Italian Cooking
  • 1986: Published Marcella's Italian Kitchen
  • 1992: Published Essentials of Classic Italian Cooking
  • 1996: Published Marcella Cucina
  • 2004: Published Marcella Says...
  • 2013: Died in Longboat Key, Florida
